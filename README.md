## 🎮 Game UI Features Explained

> Here's what you'll see on the screen while playing:

---

### 🐍 Snake Color:
🎨 Pick any color for your snake using the color picker. Want a neon green snake? Or hot pink? Your choice. The snake updates **live**.

---

### 🧱 Allow Body Pass:
🛡️ Enable or disable **self-collision**:
- ✅ **Checked** = Snake can pass through itself
- ❌ **Unchecked** = Hitting your own body ends the game

Toggle this mid-game to adjust difficulty.

---

### 🙋 Player:
🆔 You get a randomly generated name like:
```

Player: SnakenTRrJhCp

````
It’s saved in your browser using `localStorage` so it stays the same across sessions.

---

### 🧮 Score:
🎯 Tracks your current score based on how many times you eat food.

---

### 🏆 High Score:
💾 Automatically stored in your browser, even after refreshing or restarting the PC. Try to beat your personal best!

---

### 🐢 Speed:
⚡ Use the slider to increase or decrease game speed in real time (from 5 to 20 FPS).  
More speed = more thrill 😵‍💫

---

### 🌄 Game Background:
🎨 The play area (canvas) has a **light yellow** background (`#fff9c4`) for a soft, pleasing look.

---

### 🔁 Restart:
💥 Press the **Spacebar** after **Game Over** to restart instantly — no page reload needed.
